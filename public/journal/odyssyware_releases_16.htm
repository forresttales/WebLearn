Odysseyware Releases 16 Additional Career and Technical Education Courses
Latest Odysseyware expansion brings all 16 Career Clusters into the classroom

CHANDLER – Odysseyware is excited to announce 16 additional CTE courses for the 2015-16 school year. With these new courses, the company now offers courses in each of the 16 national CTE clusters. Odysseyware has a long history of providing students access to an engaging variety of CTE courses that equip them with career specific skills necessary for success in various career paths. Offering more than 80 CTE courses, students have more choices to explore than ever before.

Odysseyware leads the way in online Career and Technical Education curricula, creating the largest library of online CTE courses in the K12 market. Each course is designed by educators and experts in their respective career fields to ensure students acquire core academic and 21st century skills that will prepare them for life after graduation. The new courses are rigorous and media-rich, and cover a broad range of career paths:

• Architecture: Introduction to Architecture and Construction, Principles of Construction
• Communications: Introduction to Arts, A/V Technology and Communications, A/V Technology and Film Careers
• Government: Introduction to Government and Public Administration, Introduction to National Security
• Education: Education and Training, Careers in Teaching and Training
• Finance: Introduction to Finance, Banking Services Careers,
• Manufacturing: Principles of Manufacturing, Manufacturing Production Process Development
• Marketing: Introduction to Marketing, Principles of Marketing Research
• Logistics: Transportation, Distribution and Logistics, Logistics Planning and Management Services

These Career and Technical Education programs afford students the opportunity to explore a variety of career paths while they’re still in school. As more schools expand their CTE programs, Odysseyware remains committed to giving students as many opportunities for success as possible for life after high school.

For a complete list of courses, including the 16 released today, please visit Odysseyware online at www.odysseyware.com/career-and-technical.

About Odysseyware
Headquartered in Chandler, Arizona, Odysseyware is an innovative, multimedia-enriched online curriculum. Completely web-based, Odysseyware provides 21st century educational solutions by offering the core subjects of history and geography, math, language arts, and science along with enriching electives, CTE, placement testing, diagnostics and professional development. This curriculum is sold directly through regional education specialists.
